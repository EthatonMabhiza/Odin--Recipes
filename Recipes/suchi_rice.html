Sushi Rice

Home

You can eat this as-is or roll into your favorite sushi roll with ingredients of choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked sushi rice
  • 2 cups water
  • ¼ cup rice wine vinegar or to taste
  • ¼ cup white sugar or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Rinse rice under cold water in a strainer until water runs clear.
  3. Combine rice and water in a saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 12 minutes. Remove from heat; let stand, covered, until tender, 10 minutes more. The rice will continue to steam. Remove lid; let rice cool.
  4. Meanwhile, combine rice wine vinegar, sugar, oil, and salt in a small saucepan over medium heat; cook until sugar has dissolved. Allow to cool.
  5. Stir vinegar mixture into rice. It may look wet at first, but keep stirring; rice will dry as it cools.
  6. Have a blast!
